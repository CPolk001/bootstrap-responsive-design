Harrisonburg Fire - Station 4

Station 4, located near downtown Harrisonburg, is a unique station for both the apparatus and station setup. Sharing a station with Rockingham County/Hose Company 4, this station serves the northern end of the city, as well as downtown. Their station is separated by living quarters and office space.

Current Apparatus

Harrisonburg Station 4 houses a unique lineup of vehicles, including past and present apparatus. Station 4 is also home to Harrisonburg's parade piece, a 1954 ladder truck.

  • Engine 4, formerly Engine 23
  • Reserve Engine 9, formerly engine 26
  • Reserve Engine 23, formerly engine 23
  • Command Post unit
  • Ladder 27, HFD's ceremonial truck
