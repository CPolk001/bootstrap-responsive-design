Harrisonburg Fire - Station 3

Station 3 is located on the southeastern side of the city, servicing a large number of shopping centers and college housing. This house also has quick access to Route 33, a thoroughfare that connects to Shenandoah National Park.

Current Apparatus

Harrisonburg Station 3, similar to Station 2, is also a single engine house. Having somewhat recently received one of the city's newer trucks, this station houses the current and (occasional) reserve engine(s).

  • Engine 3, formerly Engine 26
  • Reserve Engine 9 (formerly frontline, then Reserve Engine 26)
  • Various support/chief buggies, on occasion
