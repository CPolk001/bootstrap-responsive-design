Serving the City of Harrisonburg

And the Greater Rockingham County Area


The City of Harrisonburg employs a contingent of paid Firefighters, staffing four stations and a variety of apparatus.
Although not a city-funded entity, the Harrisonburg Rescue Squad, Inc. provides medical and rescue services from many volunteers.
Both services are provided to the people of Harrisonburg, while providing mutual aid to surrounding Rockingham County.


Station Reference Site

This website is a database and reference page to identify the locations and apparatus available at each station in Harrisonburg.
Information is organized by department, Harrisonburg Fire or Harrisonburg Rescue Squad, which includes further station designation for the fire department.

Images, files, and other information provided by Google Maps, Harrisonburg Fire Department, Harrisonburg Rescue Squad, Inc., and Mike & Andrew Sanders Fire Apparatus Photography.
