Harrisonburg Rescue Squad, Inc.

The Harrisonburg Rescue Squad, Inc. is located in its own massive six bay station. Located just next to JMU's campus, this station has virtually unrestricted access to the city via Reservoir Street. The station features room for their long roster of apparatus, including space inside for rope and other various technical rescue training.
